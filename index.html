More About Us

Vision

Our vision is to foster a world where biodiversity thrives in harmony with human development, through research-driven conservation, sustainable practices, and community empowerment.

Mission

To advance biodiversity conservation through scientific research, capacity building, and community-based initiatives. We strive to promote ecological awareness, sustainable resource management, and collaborative action for protecting ecosystems and ensuring a resilient future.

Vision

To conserve biodiversity through innovative, science-based and community-driven approaches, fostering harmony between humans and nature while ensuring ecological resilience, sustainable livelihoods, and intergenerational environmental stewardship.

Mission

To protect species and habitats through research, education, and collaboration, engage communities in conservation, build awareness, promote sustainable practices, and develop leadership, ensuring ecological balance and long-term benefits for people and the planet.
